Amazon CloudWatch is a monitoring and observability service provided by AWS (Amazon Web Services). 
It helps you collect, track, visualize, and act on metrics, logs, and events
 from your AWS resources and applications.

 in simple

 CloudWatch = monitoring + loging + alarms + dashboards
